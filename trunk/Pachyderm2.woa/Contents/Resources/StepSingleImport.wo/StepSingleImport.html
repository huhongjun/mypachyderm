第1步: 导入文件

A

请在你的电脑上选择要导入的文件:

注意：当前课件只能使用如下格式的文件:

  • 图片 (.gif, .jpg)
  • 视频 (.mov, .swf)
  • 音频 (.mp3, .swf)

媒体文件指南
图片: For best results, especially for images used in zoom templates, use images that are up to 2560 pixels high and/or up to 2550 pixels wide. Smaller images will not fill the zoom template. Pachyderm will resize your image for thumbnails and smaller spots.

Video and Audio: Video files should be of standard aspect ratio (320 x 240 pixels). Video and audio files should be optimized to be as small in file size as possible; files larger than 3 or 4 MB will cause your presentation to load slowly.
